Frequently Asked Questions

What is ABHA?

ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) is a unique health ID that allows you to access and share your health records digitally. It's part of the National Digital Health Mission.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your health data is encrypted and protected. You have complete control over who can access your records, and you can revoke access at any time.

How do I update my health records?

Your health records are automatically updated when you visit any ABHA-enabled healthcare facility. You can also manually upload documents through the ABHA app.

Can I use ABHA without Aadhaar?

Yes, you can create an ABHA account using your mobile number and other government IDs. However, Aadhaar provides the most seamless experience.
